> Pay per action, not per token. Simple pricing that scales with value delivered.

## Cost-per-Step Pricing

*Currency: USD ($)*

| Mode | Cost per Step | Best For |
|:-----|:--------------|:---------|
| Flash | $0.01 | Simple tasks, quick responses |
| Fast | $0.05 | Most tasks, balanced performance |
| Max | $0.20 | Complex reasoning, multi-step workflows |

<Tip>
**Beta Phase**: During our beta phase, all other resources like computers, datastore, etc are free. You only pay for agent steps.
</Tip>

## Subscription Plans

For teams and heavy usage scenarios, we offer subscription plans with predictable monthly costs and top-up options:

- **Pay-as-you-go**: Perfect for getting started and small projects
- **Team plans**: Monthly subscriptions with included step credits
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing with volume discounts and priority support

Heavy usage users can add top-up credits to any subscription plan for seamless scaling without service interruption.

<Note>
Sign up with your work email to get **free credits automatically** and explore subscription options in your dashboard.
</Note>

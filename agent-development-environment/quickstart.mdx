---
title: 'Quickstart'
description: 'Spin up an agent in the web IDE in less than 60 seconds.'
tag: 'NEW'
---

1. Open the [Agent Development Environment](https://trypointer.com).
2. Click **ADE** in the navigation menu.
3. Type a task (e.g., *“Summarize the latest AI research on RAG.”*) and hit **Run**.
4. Watch the live stream of thoughts, tool calls, and responses.

You can replay or share the session URL with teammates for debugging.

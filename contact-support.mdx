## Response Times and Support Levels

### Community Support (Discord)
- **Response time**: Usually within a few hours during business hours
- **Best for**: General questions, implementation help, community discussions

### Email Support
- **Response time**: 24-48 hours for standard inquiries
- **Best for**: Technical issues, account questions, detailed support requests

### Enterprise Support
- **Response time**: Priority support with dedicated channels
- **Best for**: Enterprise customers with mission-critical deployments
- **Contact**: [sales@agentbase.sh](mailto:sales@agentbase.sh) for enterprise support options
